Music Mobile App
A vibrant, user-friendly music streaming prototype designed in Figma. Featuring mood-based playlists, seamless playback, and bold visual storytelling, the app reimagines how music lovers discover and enjoy songs on mobile.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

  • Project Name: Music Mobile App
  • Designer: Janani Vijay
  • Project Type: Music streaming prototype
  • Project Duration: 5 Weeks
  • Tools Used: Figma
  • Target Audience: Music lovers aged 18–35
PROBLEM STATEMENT

Music apps often feel cluttered and uninspiring, making discovery slow and frustrating. Users struggle with complex search flows, uninspired visuals, and inconsistent playback. The challenge was to design a streamlined, visually engaging experience that captures the emotional essence of music.

GOALS
  • Design a simple, fast discovery flow
  • Create mood-based personalized playlists
  • Ensure smooth, immersive playback
  • Use bold visuals with accessible contrast
USER RESEARCH
  • Surveyed 60 participants (18–35): 75% valued fast search, 65% preferred dark themes
  • 12 interviews highlighted demand for mood categories (Chill, Festival, R&B)
  • Competitive analysis: Spotify, Tidal, Deezer
DESIGN PROCESS

Step 1: Wireframes – Sketched flows for home, playlists, and player.

Step 2: Visual Design – Dark theme with purple gradients, bold typography, vibrant album art.

Step 3: Prototyping – Interactive flows in Figma (tap, swipe, playlist expansion).

KEY FEATURES
  • Personalized discovery with Daily Mixes
  • Mood-based categories like Chill, R&B, Festival
  • Unified search across songs, playlists, and artists
  • Seamless playback with shuffle, repeat, lyrics, and favorite
  • Accessibility with high-contrast UI and scalable fonts
CHALLENGES & SOLUTIONS
  • Challenge: Vibrant visuals risked clutter. Solution: Balanced gradients with clean layouts, focusing on album art.
  • Challenge: Simulating dynamic playlists in prototype. Solution: Used placeholder data and interactive states.
  • Challenge: Cross-platform consistency. Solution: Followed iOS + Material Design guidelines.
RESULTS
Final design includes 12 key screens covering the entire user journey, focusing on intuitive navigation, personalized recommendations, and seamless playback. Optimized for both iOS and Android platforms, ready for handoff to development.
LEARNINGS
  • Bold visuals enhance emotional connection with music
  • Personalized playlists improve engagement
  • Prototyping interactive flows boosts usability testing
