TRENDORA (Shopify-based)
Trendora is a Shopify-based e-commerce mobile app crafted to transform fashion and lifestyle shopping in 2024.
It offers a seamless, fast, and delightful shopping experience, making it easy for users to browse, discover, and purchase products on the go.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

  • Project Name: Trendora Mobile App
  • Designer: Janani Vijay
  • Project Type: Mobile e-commerce website (Shopify-based)
  • Project Duration: 1 Week
  • Tools Used: Figma
  • Objective: Design a simple, efficient, and user-friendly Shopify mobile app to enhance customer experience and streamline the purchase journey for fashion and lifestyle products.
PROBLEM STATEMENT

Online shopping apps often overwhelm users with too many options or complex navigation. Many users abandon carts due to poor mobile checkout flow. The challenge was to create a minimal, clean, and intuitive mobile interface that boosts engagement and conversions.

GOALS
  • Simplify the shopping experience for mobile users
  • Make product discovery and search effortless
  • Ensure a seamless checkout process
  • Build a visually appealing design aligned with modern e-commerce trends
TARGET USERS
  • Age group: 18–35
  • Tech-savvy users familiar with mobile shopping
  • Interested in fashion, accessories, and lifestyle products
  • Prefer quick, efficient, and visually appealing apps
DESIGN PROCESS

Step 1: Research – Analyzed top e-commerce apps and user pain points in mobile shopping flows.

Step 2: User Flow & Information Architecture – Designed a simple flow from Home → Category → Product → Cart → Checkout → Order Confirmation.

Step 3: Wireframes – Created low-fidelity wireframes focusing on usability and mobile-first design.

Step 4: UI Design – Selected a clean, modern design with consistent typography, minimal color palette, and touch-friendly buttons.

SCREENS DESIGNED
  • Splash / Loading Screen
  • Login / Signup Screen
  • Home / Explore Screen
  • Category / Product Listing Screen
  • Product Detail Screen
  • Cart Screen
  • Checkout / Payment Screen
  • Order Success / Confirmation Screen
  • Profile / Account Screen
  • Order History Screen
KEY FEATURES
  • Quick product search with filters and categories
  • Wishlist & Favorites for easy product tracking
  • Simplified checkout to reduce cart abandonment
  • User profile management for personalized experience
  • Order history & tracking for transparency
VISUAL DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS
  • Clean typography (Inter or Roboto)
  • Minimal, modern color palette – accent color for CTAs
  • Consistent card layouts for products
  • Large, clear buttons for touch interactions
  • Focus on product imagery and simplicity
CHALLENGES & SOLUTIONS
  • Challenge: Displaying multiple products without cluttering the screen. Solution: Card layout with ample spacing and minimal info per card.
  • Challenge: Mobile checkout complexity. Solution: Combined address, payment, and review steps into a clean, single-step flow.
  • Challenge: Maintaining brand feel while keeping it minimal. Solution: Highlighted product visuals, consistent iconography, and strategic use of accent color.
RESULTS

Final design includes 10 key screens covering the entire user journey, focusing on speed, efficiency, and usability. Ready for implementation on Shopify or as a standalone mobile web app.

LEARNINGS
  • Mobile-first design is critical for e-commerce
  • Minimal design improves usability and conversion rates
  • Consistency in UI components enhances user trust and satisfaction
